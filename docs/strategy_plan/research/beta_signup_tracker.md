## Learnings from Outreach

### What Messaging Works
- **Subject line**: [e.g., "Remember our task management rants?"]
- **Opening line**: [e.g., Personal reference to shared ISB experience]
- **Call-to-action**: [e.g., "Want early access? [link]"]

### What Messaging Doesn't Work
- [e.g., "Too salesy" or "Too long" or "No personalization"]

### Objections & How to Handle
- **Objection 1**: "I'm too busy to test beta software"
  - Response: "Just 15 min/week. I'll personally fix any bug you hit within 24 hours."
- **Objection 2**: "I'm happy with [current tool]"
  - Response: "Totally get it! Mind if I ask what you love about [tool]? Trying to learn."
- **Objection 3**: "What's in it for me?"
  - Response: "Free lifetime access + I'll build features YOU need. You get a say in roadmap."
