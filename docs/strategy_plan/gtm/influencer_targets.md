## Target Criteria

**Ideal influencer profile**:
- 10K-100K subscribers/followers (micro-influencers, not mega stars)
- Productivity/task management niche
- Audience: PMs, founders, knowledge workers (our ICP)
- Authentic voice (not overly promotional)
- Previous reviews of Motion/Reclaim/Sunsama (knows the space)

**Why micro-influencers**:
- Higher engagement rates than mega influencers (5-10% vs. 1-2%)
- More affordable ($500 vs. $5,000)
- Audience trusts them more (not celebrity endorsements)
- Better ROI (500 signups from $500 = $1 CPA)

## Affiliate Program (Alternative to Sponsorship)

**Offer**:
- 20% commission on first 12 months of paid subscriptions
- Free Pro tier for influencer
- Custom affiliate link with tracking

**Why influencers say yes**:
- No upfront payment needed (performance-based)
- Ongoing revenue (not one-time $500)
- Aligns incentives (they want their audience to use it)

**Example**:
- Influencer refers 100 users
- 10 convert to Indie tier ($5/mo) + 5 convert to Pro tier ($15/mo)
- Monthly commission: (10 x $5 x 0.2) + (5 x $15 x 0.2) = $10 + $15 = $25/mo
- 12-month commission: $300 (vs. $500 one-time sponsorship)

**Trade-off**: Lower upfront cost, but slower payout. Good for Phase 2 (budget-constrained).

## Red Flags (Influencers to Avoid)

**Don't approach if**:
- Engagement rate <2% (bought followers)
- Only does sponsored content (no organic reviews)
- Audience mismatch (students, not knowledge workers)
- Negative reviews of similar tools (won't be authentic)
- Too expensive (>$1,000 for <100K subs)
