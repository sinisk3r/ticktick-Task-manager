## Final Notes

### Philosophy
- **Ship fast, learn faster**: Beta doesn't have to be perfect. It has to be good enough to learn from.
- **Ruthless prioritization**: Only build features that directly support the "Aha Moment" (avoidance detection + task breakdown).
- **Honest communication**: Tell beta users "This is rough. Help me make it great."
- **Measure what matters**: Retention > Features. If users don't come back, nothing else matters.

### Mindset for Nights & Weekends
- **Protect your energy**: 15-20 hours/week is sustainable. 40 hours/week leads to burnout.
- **Focus on code, not meetings**: Limit calls to customer interviews. No status meetings.
- **Batch similar tasks**: Code on weeknights, customer calls on weekends.
- **Celebrate small wins**: Shipped a feature? Fixed a bug? High-five yourself.

### When to Adjust the Plan
- **Add 1 week buffer** if critical bugs emerge during Week 3
- **Extend to 6 weeks** if co-founder drops out
- **Pivot immediately** if Week 1 interviews reveal a broken hypothesis
- **Kill fast** if Week 4 retention is 0% (don't throw good time after bad)
